CHOCOLATE FUDGE🍫🍩☕😋

Small 🍫 bites ❤️

INGREDIENTS

  • Milk
  • Condensed Milk
  • Cooking Chocolate
  • Coconut Milk

RECIPE

  1. Melt the chocolate.
  2. Mix condensed milk, Milk and Coconut milk.
  3. Pour the batter in flat plate.
  4. Refrigerate for 2 hours.
  5. Cut into square pieces.
  6. Enjoy!🍴🍴
